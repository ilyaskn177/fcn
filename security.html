Security FAQs

Common questions about FCN's security measures

How does FCN protect my financial data?

FCN uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all financial transactions and data storage. We implement tokenization for sensitive payment information and conduct regular security audits to maintain the highest protection standards.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?

Immediately change your password and enable MFA if not already active. Contact our security team at security@fcnapp.com and review your recent account activity for any suspicious transactions.

Does FCN store my banking details?

We use tokenization to securely process payments without storing your full banking details on our servers. Only the necessary payment tokens required for transaction processing are retained.

How often does FCN conduct security audits?

We perform internal security audits quarterly and engage independent third-party auditors annually. Additionally, we conduct continuous automated vulnerability scanning and penetration testing.

Have More Security Questions?

Our security team is available 24/7 to address any concerns you may have.
